👋 Meet the Founder

Hi! I’m Romesa, the creator behind Buttoned Up.

I run every part of the business, from designing the pins to marketing and selling them. I started this business because I enjoy being creative and wanted to turn that into something fun that others can enjoy too.

I like keeping up with trends and creating designs that people actually want to wear. My goal is to keep growing Buttoned Up and continue making new and unique pins for everyone.
