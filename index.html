Who We Are 🌈

Buttoned Up is a creative, youth-run small business that designs unique button pins inspired by safe, original, trendy, aesthetic, and expressive themes.

🎨

Creative Designs

Each pin is made to stand out with bright colors, fun layouts, and expressive styles that show personality.

🧷

Easy to Wear

Our pins are lightweight and easy to attach to backpacks, jackets, tote bags, pencil cases, lanyards, and more.

💝

Perfect Gifts

Button pins are affordable, cute, and meaningful gifts for friends, classmates, family, clubs, and school events.
